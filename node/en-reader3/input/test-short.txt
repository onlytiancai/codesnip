Once upon a time, in a faraway kingdom, there lived a beautiful young girl named Cinderella. She lived with her stepmother and two stepsisters, who were very cruel to her. Every day, Cinderella had to do all the household chores - cleaning, cooking, washing, and sewing - while her stepsisters did nothing but dress up in fine clothes and attend parties.

One day, an invitation arrived from the palace. The King was hosting a grand ball so that the Prince could find a bride. The stepsisters were overjoyed and immediately began preparing their most beautiful gowns and jewelry.
